About Us

The Association Of Table Water Operators and Technicians (ATWOPAT) is a national professional body established to unite individuals working within the water packaging and production industry across Nigeria.

The association brings together Operators, Sachet Water Baggers, Technicians, and Technical Experts specializing in various aspects of production, including:

  • Water treatment and Reverse Osmosis (RO) systems
  • Sachet water production machines
  • Dispenser packaging machines
  • Monoblock filling machines
  • PET blowing machines
  • Shrink wrapping & sealing machines
  • And other related production, and packaging equipments.

ATWOPAT serves as a central platform where members can connect, collaborate, and contribute to the advancement of the industry through shared experience and technical expertise.

Core Purpose

  • To unite water packaging operators and technicians nationwide
  • To create a structured platform for sharing job opportunities and empowerments within the industry
  • To encourage knowledge exchange on water quality, machine operations, maintenance, and troubleshooting
  • To support continuous learning, training, and technical skill development
  • To represent and promote the collective interests of operators and technicians
  • To provide solutions to operational and production challenges
  • To encourage professional communication between operators and management
  • To promote safety, efficiency, and industry best practices
  • To facilitate access to identification cards, training programs, and certification
  • To advocate for worker welfare, fair compensation, and improved working conditions

Members of ATWOPAT gain access to a professional network that supports career growth, technical improvement, and industry recognition. The association provides opportunities for learning, collaboration, and participation in decisions that impact operators and technicians across the country.

Code of Conduct And Professional Standards

ATWOPAT is built on discipline, respect, and professionalism. All members are expected to uphold the association’s code of conduct, which is designed to maintain order, credibility, and mutual respect within the community.

Failure to adhere to these standards may result in disciplinary actions, as the association prioritizes integrity and the collective reputation of its members.

Every member is required to take these guidelines seriously to ensure a productive and professional environment.

Disclaimer

ATWOPAT is a professional association of operators and technicians.

It is not a government body but an independent organization committed to advancing the interests and standards of professionals within the water packaging industry.
