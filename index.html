About Us

ATWOPAT is a national professional body uniting individuals across Nigeria’s water packaging industry.

  • Water treatment & RO systems
  • Sachet water machines
  • Dispenser packaging machines
  • Monoblock filling machines
  • PET blowing machines
  • Shrink wrapping & sealing machines

We provide a platform for collaboration, technical exchange, and industry advancement.

Core Purpose

  • Unite operators and technicians nationwide
  • Share job opportunities and empowerment
  • Promote training and skill development
  • Support troubleshooting and knowledge exchange
  • Advocate for welfare and fair compensation
  • Encourage safety and best practices

Code of Conduct

ATWOPAT is built on discipline, respect, and professionalism. Members must uphold standards to maintain credibility and integrity.
